National Bird of Nepal

Lophophorus Impejanus (Danfe)

The Himalaya Monal, Lophophorus Impejanus also called Danfe.
It is the genus Lophophorus of peaseant faimly, Phasianidae.
It is the National Bird of Nepal. It is the beautifully colourful peaseant.

There are different characterstics of Male and Female as Mentioned Below.

  1. Characteristics of Male
  2. Characteristics of Female

1. Characteristics of Male
  • The male is 12 colors spectacular combinations.
  • The male include a long, metallic green crest, coppery feathers on the back and neck, and a prominent white rump that is most visible when the bird is in flight.

2. Characteristics of Female.
  • The females are earthy brown and duller in color
  • The female has a prominent white patch on the throat and a white strip on the tail.
